# Product
The product consists of 5 different interactive dashboards that allow analysing different Black owned banks, specifically investing in low-income communities and compare those to major financial institutions in the USA. 

## Dashboards:
  * [Overall Statistics](https://tableau.dsc.umich.edu/t/UM-Public/views/BankingBlack/MDI-OverallStats?:iid=1&:isGuestRedirectFromVizportal=y&:embed=y)
  * [Small Business Loans](https://tableau.dsc.umich.edu/t/UM-Public/views/BankingBlack/MDI-SmallBusLoans?%3Aiid=1&%3AisGuestRedirectFromVizportal=y&%3Aembed=y)
  * [Home Mortage Loans](https://tableau.dsc.umich.edu/t/UM-Public/views/BankingBlack/MDI-HomeMortLoans?%3Aiid=1&%3AisGuestRedirectFromVizportal=y&%3Aembed=y)
  * [Investing in Low Income Communities](https://tableau.dsc.umich.edu/t/UM-Public/views/BankingBlack/MDI-LoaningEfforts?%3Aiid=1&%3AisGuestRedirectFromVizportal=y&%3Aembed=y)
  * [Comparison](https://tableau.dsc.umich.edu/t/UM-Public/views/BankingBlack/BB-MDIComparison?%3Aiid=1&%3AisGuestRedirectFromVizportal=y&%3Aembed=y)
